What Will Shape the Next Generation of SDVs?

The future of mobility is not a single technology—it is seamless integration across hardware, software, networking, safety, security, and cloud.

Thesis: E/E + Software Platform as the Multiplier

Among all architectures, the shift to zonal / centralized E/E combined with a modern software platform (AUTOSAR Adaptive, SOA, CI/CD, OTA) will have the largest systemic impact. It unlocks continuous feature delivery, consolidates AI compute, reduces wiring cost/weight, and makes security and data pipelines practical at scale.

Biggest impact = E/E transformation (Zonal → Centralized HPC) × Software Architecture (SOA + continuous delivery) — with Security and Functional safety as non-negotiable foundations.

Why Not Only ADAS or Connectivity?

  • ADAS delivers user-visible autonomy value, but depends on compute, networking, and safety architecture.
  • Connectivity/OTA enables post-sale value, but needs secure E/E and software lifecycle to be trustworthy.
  • E/E + Software is the substrate that multiplies every other domain’s effectiveness.

Collaboration Differentiator

As the industry moves toward centralized computing, zonal architectures, AI-enabled vehicles, and continuous software delivery, cross-domain collaboration becomes the key OEM/supplier differentiator.
