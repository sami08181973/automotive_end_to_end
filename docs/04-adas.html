ADAS & Autonomous Architecture

Perception, sensor fusion, decision-making, and automated driving functions (L0–L4).

ASIL-DPerceptionFusionPlanning

Pipeline

  1. Sense – Camera, Radar, LiDAR, Ultrasonic, IMU, GNSS
  2. Perceive – Detect objects, lanes, free space
  3. Fuse – Multi-sensor object list + confidence
  4. Decide – ACC/LKA/Highway Pilot/AEB actions
  5. Act – Torque/brake/steer requests to chassis & powertrain
Sensors → Perception → Fusion → Decision → Actuation ↓ Safety Monitor (ASIL-D)
